Careers in Computer Science

The field of computer science is continuously growing, with software developers, security analysts, web developers, and other specialized occupations in high demand as computer technology and the internet continue to shape and influence our global culture and economy. Computer science occupations also generally pay higher than average and only require a bachelor's or associate's degree for an entry-level position.

Many tech companies try to build fun and engaging work environments, and offer competitive benefits including insurance plans, on-site gyms, 401k investment plans, and multiple weeks of paid vacation. Most organizations pride themselves on their company culture and value teamwork, which is essential to achieving success on large software projects.

As seen in the table below, jobs in software development, information security, and web development are expected to grow significantly over the coming years. In fact, the number of open positions is growing faster than the number of qualified applicants, so there are plentiful opportunities in a variety of different areas within computer science.
